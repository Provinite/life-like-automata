Class GameBoard

Represents a two-dimensional orthogonal grid of square cells that have two states. Indices are in (row, column) order and begin in the top-left corner at (0,0). Use the fromBooleanArray or fromSize factory methods to get an instance.

________________________
| 0,0 | 0,1 | 0,2 | 0,3|
| 1,0 | 1,1 | 1,2 | 1,3|
| 2,0 | 2,1 | 2,2 | 2,3|
| 3,0 | 3,1 | 3,2 | 3,3|
------------------------
